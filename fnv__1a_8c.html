Compute an FNV-1a 64-bit hash over the given buffer.

A region of the buffer can be excluded from the hash, by specifying its starting position in skip_pos and its length in skip_len.

Parameters
bufThe buffer.
lenThe length of buf.
skip_posThe beginning of the region of buf to exclude from the hash. Can be 0.
skip_lenThe length of the region of buf to exclude from the hash. Can be 0.
Returns
The FNV-1a 64-bit hash of buffer, excluding the skip region.
